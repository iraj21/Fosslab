Here the cgpa for the first year of students of class CSB is calculated.
The script initially requires 3 files namely :- s1.pdf s2.pdf c4b.txt.
Here s1.pdf and s2.pdf are downloaded from the ktu website and c4b.txt is downloaded from the given link http://14.139.184.212/ask/c4b/c4b.txt
From c4b the roll numbers are extracted and is used for the seperating CSB students from the CS department.
